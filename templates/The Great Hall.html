The Great Hall

Location

Situated in Sydney Boys High School on 556 Cleveland St, Moore Park NSW 2021, the great hall is adjacent to Fox Studios and fashionable Crown & Bourke Streets.

Public Transportation

Public transportation to and from The Great Hall is quite convenient, with bus stops on both Cleveland Street and Anzac Pde, directly next to the location. There is also a Light Rail stop right next to the location, Moore Park Light Rail stop.

Features

Outside the hall on the west side there is a charming, enclosed garden with colonnades which provide the perfect setting for pre-dinner cocktails and has ample space for a bar and live music. On the east side of The Great Hall a balcony runs the length of the Hall with garden views.

Features include:

  • Place of Public Entertainment Licence
  • Disabled access
  • 72 square metre stage
  • No noise restrictions
  • Sound system
  • Grand piano
  • ample chairs for any event
  • Heating
  • Large, tiered gallery level
  • Projection room overlooking the Hall and stage
  • Kitchen facility
  • Break out rooms for large conferences or conventions

Parking

Vehicular access is from the Sir Roden Cutler Gates (Gate 9) Anzac Parade, Surry Hills. There are ample parking spots subject to approval from the Venues Manager for use.
